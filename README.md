# SAKHI+

SAKHI+ is a women-centric digital platform designed to empower women
through health awareness, safety support, career guidance, and
community building.

## Problem Statement
Women often lack access to a unified, safe, and reliable digital
platform that supports their health, safety, career growth, and
emotional well-being.

## Solution
SAKHI+ provides a single ecosystem that integrates AI-powered guidance,
emergency safety features, educational resources, and a supportive
community for women.
